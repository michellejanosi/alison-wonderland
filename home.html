biography



Alison Wonderland is a well known Australian producer and DJ originating from Sydney. The singer/DJ signed to EMI Music Australia which is a part of Universal Music Australia. Her real name is Alex Sholler but is known worldwide as Alison Wonderland. The 30 year old DJ made her debut with the single “Get Ready” in 2013 which gained popularity instantly. In 2016, her song “Run” appeared at number 59 on Triple J’s Hottest 100. She was also nominated for two ARIA awards in 2015 for best dance title and best video.


Wonderland has performed in LA at the official Grammy's Afterparty, all throughout Ibiza on top of MOS and the Brit Awards Afterparty in London. In Australia she has torn up main stages at Splendour in the Grass, Falls Festival, Southbound Festival, Stereosonic, Field Day, Parklife (nationally), Good Vibrations Festival, Harbourlife, Sydney Fashion Week, Future Music Festival, Coaster Festival, Secret Garden Festival and more, not to mention her sellout headline national tours.


Alison Wonderland is the party. She has a cult following that has propelled her, and the ridiculously good dance music she makes, into the spotlight.
